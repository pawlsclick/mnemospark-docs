## Summary (from page metadata)

Agentic AI systems can execute workflows, make decisions, and coordinate across departments. To realize its promise, companies must:

- **Design workflows around outcomes** and appoint **mission owners** who define the mission, steer both humans and AI agents, and own the outcome
- **Unlock the data silos** it needs to access and **clarify the business logic** underpinning it
- **Develop the leaders and guardrails** that these intelligent systems require

Companies have often responded to new technologies with process tweaks or bolt-on tools. Some companies adapted; others faltered, not for lack of innovation but because they failed to reorganize around it. Agentic AI raises the same challenge but with higher stakes. It demands cross-functional execution that breaks free from silos and outdated ways of working.
